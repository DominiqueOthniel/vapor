# Vape Store E-commerce Design System

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) - Professional, sophisticated base
- **Secondary**: Warm copper (#b87333) - Premium accent for CTAs and highlights
- **Tertiary**: Soft sage (#9caf88) - Calming secondary accent
- **Neutral**: Warm white (#fafafa) - Clean background
- **Text**: High contrast dark gray (#2d2d2d) for readability

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Canela (serif) - Bold, editorial feel for headings and hero text
- **Body Font**: Suisse Int'l (sans-serif) - Clean, modern readability for product descriptions and UI text
- **Accent Font**: Space Mono (monospace) - Technical precision for product specs and pricing

### Visual Language
- **Minimalist Luxury**: Clean lines, generous whitespace, premium feel
- **Editorial Aesthetic**: Magazine-quality product presentation
- **Technical Precision**: Clear product specifications and detailed imagery
- **Trust & Safety**: Professional presentation that builds confidence

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and cart animations
- **Splide.js**: Elegant product carousels and image galleries
- **ECharts.js**: Clean data visualization for product comparisons
- **p5.js**: Subtle background particle effects for visual interest
- **Matter.js**: Physics-based interactions for product demos
- **Pixi.js**: High-performance visual effects for hero sections
- **Shader-park**: Sophisticated background shaders

### Animation & Effects
- **Text Effects**: 
  - Color cycling emphasis on product names
  - Split-by-letter stagger for hero headlines
  - Subtle gradient text animation on CTAs
- **Image Effects**:
  - Ken Burns pan/zoom on hero images
  - Product image galleries with smooth transitions
  - Hover-activated 3D tilt effects
- **Background Effects**:
  - Subtle particle system using p5.js
  - Liquid-metal displacement shaders for premium feel
  - Aurora gradient flow for hero sections

### Header & Navigation
- **Fixed Navigation**: Clean, minimal header with cart icon and badge
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Seamless page navigation with scroll indicators
- **Search Integration**: Prominent search bar with real-time suggestions
- **Cart Dropdown**: Elegant slide-down cart preview with item thumbnails

### Product Display
- **Grid Layout**: Responsive product grid with hover effects
- **Quick View**: Modal overlays for detailed product information
- **Image Zoom**: High-resolution product images with magnification
- **Stock Indicators**: Real-time availability with color-coded status

### Interactive Elements
- **Filter System**: Multi-criteria filtering with smooth animations
- **Cart Management**: Real-time quantity adjustments with visual feedback
- **Product Comparison**: Side-by-side feature comparison tool
- **Wishlist Toggle**: Heart icon animations for saved products

### Mobile Responsiveness
- **Touch-Friendly**: Large tap targets and gesture support
- **Optimized Layouts**: Vertical product cards and simplified navigation
- **Performance**: Lightweight animations and optimized images
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios and screen reader support

## Content Strategy
- **Professional Product Photography**: Clean, consistent product images
- **Detailed Specifications**: Comprehensive product information
- **Brand Storytelling**: Premium brand positioning and trust signals
- **User Experience**: Intuitive navigation and seamless checkout flow
